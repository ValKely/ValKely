Smart Pricing Analytics for E-Commerce Sellers

Uncovering the Key Drivers of Smartphone Discounts — A FinTech-Savvy Approach to Pricing Intelligence

Dynamic pricing decisions on e-commerce platforms are often made without deep visibility into competitive trends. I built a lightweight data scraping tool using BeautifulSoup to extract smartphone listings from Jumia for analysis. By examining variables like brand, condition, customer ratings, and discount levels, this project reveals what drives price markdowns — helping digital B2C sellers craft more data-informed pricing strategies and enhance profitability.

Business Impact

Cracking the Discount Code

At first glance, Jumia's discounts may look random. But behind the scenes, clear patterns reveal how pricing decisions are made. Understanding these patterns is like decoding a fintech pricing algorithm — vital for businesses aiming to optimize customer acquisition and retention.

Key Influencers in Pricing

Brand reputation, product condition, customer ratings, and product type all play their role — much like how fintech products adjust offers based on user credit scores, usage behavior, or SaaS platforms tailor pricing tiers by feature demand.

Driving Smarter Sales Strategies

By grasping these pricing drivers, sellers gain the power to fine-tune discounts and promotions — an approach that mirrors dynamic pricing models in fintech and subscription optimizations in SaaS, ultimately boosting sales and customer satisfaction.

My Role & Process

Data Scraping

Using Python, I scraped thousands of smartphone listings on Jumia, pulling vital info like brand names, ratings, price points, and discount percentages — much like gathering transaction or customer usage data for fintech or SaaS analytics.

Data Cleaning

Raw data is messy. I normalized inconsistent entries, filtered out duplicates, and extracted discount signals, ensuring accuracy and reliability — foundational steps for any data-driven fintech or SaaS solution.

Visualization & Insight

With Power BI, I built intuitive dashboards that highlight key correlations — empowering stakeholders to see, at a glance, how factors like product type and brand sway discount rates, similar to fintech risk dashboards or SaaS customer engagement reports.

Key Features

Data-Driven Pricing Insights
